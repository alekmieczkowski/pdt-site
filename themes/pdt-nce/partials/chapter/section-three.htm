  • December 8th, 2013

    Induction

    One-year prior, 44 men all worked together to start a chapter of Phi Delta Theta here at UNC Charlotte. Though they enjoyed the years work with a celebration among close friends, family, and alumni; these men knew that this was only the beginning of a tough but successful future.

  • March 20th, 2014

    Charter & Initiation

    This was it. After 2 years of hard work they had finally done it. This was the day that those 44 men became not only brothers, but founding fathers of the NC Epsilon Chapter. This was their greatest achievement yet, however these men strived to aim higher; Setting a new standard that crafted our fraternity into what it is today.

  • November 28th, 2015

    First Brothers in IFC Exec

    November 28th marked an important step in our chapters history. This was the day we finally had our organization up to our own standards. Due to the hard work of the founding class, we were finally ready to join the IFC executive board and show our new Greek community that are willing to step up and help in the Intra Fraternal Counsel.

  • April 15th, 2016

    First Airband

    After a couple of years of growing our chapter, we decided that it was finally time to show UNC Charlotte what we were capable of. We entered our first Airband competition with an act titled “The Evolution of Drake”. Though we did not place, we set a new standard by creating our own Snapchat filter that was recognized school wide.

  • April 16th, 2016

    Third Place in First Greek Games

    Greek games has been a staple of competition between different Greek organizations since it was founded. As a new chapter with no prior experience, we created teams for each event. In the end, we placed 3rd in our very first Greek games with our speed pyramid team winning 1st place, and our volleyball and tug-of-war teams placing high enough to ensure our overall standing.
